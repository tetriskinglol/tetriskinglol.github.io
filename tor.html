⚠️ Legal Warning

Tor is designed for privacy and anonymity.

Using Tor to access illegal content or purchase illegal items is against the law.

Always use Tor responsibly and legally.

What is Tor?

Tor (The Onion Router) is a network that allows users to browse the internet anonymously by routing traffic through multiple servers.

How it works

Your Device → Node 1 → Node 2 → Node 3 → Website

Each step encrypts your data, hiding your identity.

What it is used for

  • Privacy and anonymity
  • Bypassing censorship
  • Protecting personal data

Important Notes

  • Tor does not make you completely untraceable
  • Do not log into personal accounts if you want anonymity
  • Always follow laws in your country

Why it's used

Tor is used by journalists, researchers, and privacy-focused users to protect their identity and communications online.
