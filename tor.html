Using Tor is legal in most countries, but using it for illegal activities is not. Do not attempt to buy illegal goods or access illegal content.

What is Tor?

Tor (The Onion Router) is a network that helps you stay anonymous online by routing your internet traffic through multiple servers.

How it Works

  • Your traffic is encrypted
  • It passes through multiple nodes
  • Each node only knows the previous and next step
  • This makes tracking very difficult

Common Uses

  • Privacy protection
  • Bypassing censorship
  • Anonymous browsing

Important Notes

  • Tor does not make you completely invisible
  • You can still be tracked if you log into accounts
  • Always use it responsibly
